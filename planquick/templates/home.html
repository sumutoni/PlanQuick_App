What is PlanQuick?

PlanQuick is a platform that allows you to account for your mobile banking transactions. We provide you with tools to vizualize your mobile banking data.

We as planquick recognize the difficulty in accountability of mobile transactions and are here to provide you with a user friendly solution to that. Our aim is to expand the platform to allow you to create your own budgets and keep track of all your spending habits.

How It works

Planquick provides you with an easy to use interface where you can upload your data. We'll use that data to provide you with your spending and received transactions in some easy to read charts. We will also provide sub-totals of your transactions in different categories.
